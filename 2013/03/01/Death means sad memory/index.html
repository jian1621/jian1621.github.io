Death means Sad Memory.

发表于 2013-03-01   |   分类于 催眠   |  

Three persons who are very close to me had gone in this year. I hope to writesomething to memory them using my poor english.

In April,I was lunching with my friend in canteen when i heard that you died because of the high blood pressure. I was so astounding that i forgot i was eating at that moment.My friend had found something wrong and asked me what happened. I didn’t know what could i say. I just feel sad. I still remember the day you visited my family in the last spring festival with older sister. It’s just four months from then,just one hundred and twenty days passed , one cousin of mine has gone. You were so young that i was very worried about my aunt. Could she suffer this kind fo sorrow? After all, she is more than sisty-years old. I know that we would talk something interesting together in the next spring festival in my home. However, you won’t be there.

Half In September,I was studying vc++ in lab. A call brought me message of the murder in BeiJing. Two men were killed, one is you. I rellay can’t believe the fact.I asked myself again and again to avoid the truth, but ,you died ,at the age of twenty-five. A time just like the season when the flower are bloom.Maybe, someone in our village said you were not a good boy,you fighted with some others in the school,you didn’t got a good grade, you got a strange haircut. However, I don’t agree. I don’t know whether you still remember that i had accept the courage from you when i was suffering serious depression in high school. I used to hope that i could join in activities with you. You gave me a feeling like my brother. I got back home in this national holiday, and I saw your grandma bicycling to the field. She is so silent that felt me sad. I didn’t know how to comfort her.

In October, my youngest aunt died for the mammary cancer .Just like a strike to my heart. It has been a very long time since the last time I visited her as I am studying outside where were always very far away from my hometown. I can’t even remember the road to her home. But those days I played in her home when I was young are so happy,even we were all very poor then. Sometimes, I prepared to take a picture with her if possible. However, I didn’t have chance when I got back home because of kinds reasons. Now, that won’t be true. My little cousin became a boy who losted his mother when he was fourteen. Hope he be strong.

Until now I had never wept. But I always woke up with missing of you in nights.
